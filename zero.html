FAVOURITE BAND

BURUKLYN BOYZ

Buruklyn Boyz members Ajay and Mr. Right have risen to be one of the most searched and recognized drill music recording artists from Kenya. Recently, Buruklyn Boyz released their debut album, ‘East Mpaka London’ which solidified their musical career. Before the release of their debut album, Buruklyn Boyz had released singles that made them internationally recognized. Let us loo at the Buruklyn Boyz members and their roles in the group.

Ajay Buruklyn Boyz Biography

Ajay real name is Jayson Okanda Omondi (@buruklynboy1 on Instagram), is one of the founders of Buruklyn Boyz. He is best known for his lyricism. For example, in the East Mpaka London album, Ajay is praised for his chorus in the song “Pree.”

Mr Rght Buruklyn Boyz Biography

Mr. Right real name is Malvin Ng’ang’a ( @mrright_ke on Instagram), is an artist, a rapper, and a performer from Kenya. He is a member of the Buruklyn Boyz. They founded the record label Buruklyn Boyz with Ajay, which has grown to become one of Kenya’s largest music labels.

Ali Buddz Buruklyn Boyz Manager.

Ali Buddz (@Ali_buddz on Instagram) is a creative director, Buruklyn Boyz manager, a third of Urban Pitchaz, and an entrepreneur based in Buru Buru Nairobi. Buddz joined Buruklyn Boyz record label as the group’s CEO, manager, creative director, and advisor. Major Buruklyn Boyz’s success is attributed to Ali Buddz, who has helped in executive roles such as organizing shows, appearances, and performances.
